# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Participants in this project are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional
- Focus on technical substance
- Assume good intent
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or abusive language
- Trolling, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated off-topic disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation for violations of this code.

For serious concerns, contact:

- `support@logister.org`
